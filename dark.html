Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our platform

How do I create an account?

+

Creating an account is simple! Click on the "Get Started" button at the top of the page, fill in your basic information, and verify your email address. Once verified, you can complete your profile and start applying for jobs immediately.

Is there a cost to use FITLY FIND?

+

No, FITLY FIND is completely free for job seekers. Employers pay to post jobs and access premium features, but as a candidate, you can search, apply, and connect with employers at no cost.

How can I improve my profile visibility?

+

To maximize your visibility to employers:

  • Complete all sections of your profile (100% completion)
  • Add relevant skills and certifications
  • Upload a professional resume
  • Keep your profile active by applying to jobs regularly
  • Enable "Open to Opportunities" in your settings

Can I apply for jobs without a resume?

+

Yes! Our platform allows you to apply using your profile information. However, we recommend uploading a resume as many employers prefer to review traditional resumes. You can easily upload and manage multiple resume versions in your profile.

How do I know if an employer viewed my application?

+

You'll receive notifications when:

  • Your application is received
  • An employer views your profile
  • Your application status changes
You can also track all your applications in the "My Applications" section of your dashboard.

What types of jobs are available on FITLY FIND?

+

We feature opportunities across all industries and experience levels, including:

  • Full-time positions
  • Part-time jobs
  • Remote and hybrid roles
  • Internships
  • Freelance/contract work
  • Executive positions
Use our advanced filters to find exactly what you're looking for.

Still have questions? Contact our support team and we'll be happy to help!
