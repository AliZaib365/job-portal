How Our Matching Algorithm Works

Intelligent technology that connects the right talent with the right opportunities

1

Job Seekers Upload Profiles

Candidates create detailed profiles with skills, experience, and preferences, plus upload their CVs for analysis.

2

Employers Post Requirements

Companies post detailed job descriptions specifying required skills, experience level, and cultural fit needs.

3

Intelligent Pattern Matching

Our AI analyzes both sides for compatibility across multiple dimensions including skills, experience, culture, and values.

4

Instant Match Notifications

When we find strong matches, both parties receive notifications with compatibility scores and match details.
